## Core Message

**Summary:**

Agile Testing shows that quality is a team responsibility, not an isolated QA activity. Testers, developers, and product people must collaborate from the beginning.

The book emphasizes automated testing, fast feedback, and shared ownership for product quality.

---

## Collaboration Principles

- Test early and often.
- Use examples to clarify requirements.
- Automate regression tests.
- Make testing part of each sprint.

---

## Practical Steps

- [ ] Involve testers in planning.
- [ ] Define acceptance criteria with the team.
- [ ] Automate the test suite.
- [ ] Review quality goals every sprint.

## Notes

The main takeaway is that agile development needs testing practices built into the team's workflow, not bolted on at the end.
