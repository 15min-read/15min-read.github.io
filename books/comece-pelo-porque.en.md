## Main Idea

**Summary:**

Simon Sinek explains that inspiring leaders and brands start with "why" — their purpose, cause, or belief. People buy into why you do something, not just what you do or how you do it.

The most successful organizations communicate from the inside out: Why → How → What.

---

## The Golden Circle

- Why: your core purpose.
- How: the actions or processes that deliver that purpose.
- What: the products or services you offer.

When why is clear, customers and employees connect emotionally with your message.

---

## Practical Checklist

- [ ] Define your core purpose in one sentence.
- [ ] Explain how your work supports that purpose.
- [ ] Describe what you do in tangible terms.
- [ ] Share your why before the what.
