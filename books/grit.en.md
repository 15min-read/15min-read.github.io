## What is Grit?

**Summary:**

Angela Duckworth shows that passion and perseverance matter more than talent alone. Grit is the sustained effort toward long-term goals, even in the face of setbacks.

The book is based on research across athletes, students, and professionals. It argues that grit predicts success better than pure intelligence or skill.

---

## Four Components

- Interest: a long-term fascination.
- Practice: deliberate effort to improve.
- Purpose: believing your work matters.
- Hope: belief that effort makes progress.

---

## Practical Checklist

- [ ] Identify one long-term goal you care about.
- [ ] Practice deliberately with clear feedback.
- [ ] Connect your task to a larger purpose.
- [ ] Keep a learning mindset through challenges.
