## Fixed vs Growth Mindset

**Summary:**

Carol Dweck differentiates between a fixed mindset, which believes abilities are static, and a growth mindset, which believes skills can be developed through effort.

A growth mindset encourages learning from failure, embracing challenge, and seeing effort as a path to improvement. It is not a magic solution, but a powerful way to interpret setbacks and success.

---

## Practical Implications

- Praise effort and strategy, not innate talent.
- View mistakes as learning opportunities.
- Choose challenges that stretch your abilities.
- Maintain curiosity and persistence.
